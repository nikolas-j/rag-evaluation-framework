ACME CORPORATION INFORMATION SECURITY POLICY
Effective Date: March 15, 2025
Policy Version: 2.1
Owner: Chief Information Security Officer (CISO)

PURPOSE
This policy establishes security requirements to protect ACME Corporation's information assets, systems, and data.

PASSWORD REQUIREMENTS

1. Complexity Standards
   - Minimum length: 12 characters
   - Must include: uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters
   - Cannot contain username or company name
   - Cannot reuse last 10 passwords

2. Password Management
   - Passwords must be changed every 90 days
   - Maximum login attempts before account lockout: 5 attempts
   - Account lockout duration: 30 minutes
   - Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is required for:
     * VPN access
     * Email access from external networks
     * Administrative accounts
     * Financial systems

DATA CLASSIFICATION

Level 1 - Public: Information approved for public release
Level 2 - Internal: General business information for internal use only
Level 3 - Confidential: Sensitive business data requiring protection
Level 4 - Restricted: Highly sensitive data (PII, financial records, trade secrets)

Level 4 data must be encrypted both at rest and in transit using AES-256 encryption.

ACCEPTABLE USE POLICY

Permitted Activities:
- Business-related internet browsing
- Professional development and research
- Limited personal use during breaks (less than 30 minutes per day)

Prohibited Activities:
- Downloading or distributing copyrighted material without authorization
- Accessing, storing, or transmitting illegal, offensive, or discriminatory content
- Installing unauthorized software or applications
- Using company resources for personal business ventures
- Sharing credentials or allowing unauthorized access to systems

DEVICE SECURITY

Corporate Devices:
- Full disk encryption required on all laptops and mobile devices
- Automatic screen lock after 10 minutes of inactivity
- Devices must be physically secured when unattended
- Lost or stolen devices must be reported to IT Security within 2 hours

BYOD (Bring Your Own Device):
- Personal devices accessing corporate email must have:
  * Device passcode/biometric authentication
  * Remote wipe capability enabled
  * Latest operating system security updates
- Corporate data must be stored only in approved applications

INCIDENT REPORTING
All security incidents must be reported to the Security Operations Center (SOC) immediately:
- Email: security@acme.corp
- Hotline: 1-800-555-SECURE (24/7)
- Internal Extension: 7911

Response time targets:
- Critical incidents (data breach, ransomware): 15 minutes
- High priority incidents: 1 hour
- Medium priority incidents: 4 hours
- Low priority incidents: 24 hours

TRAINING AND COMPLIANCE
- Annual security awareness training is mandatory for all employees
- Phishing simulation tests conducted quarterly
- Policy acknowledgment required within 30 days of hire and annually thereafter
- Violations may result in disciplinary action including termination

POLICY REVIEW
This policy is reviewed and updated annually or as needed based on emerging threats and regulatory requirements.
